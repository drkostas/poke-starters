Real interactive demo: spin the reels, walk the ranked results with Next, and play with every parameter (each one drives its own animation). Sprites are authentic PokeAPI FR-LG / RBY game sprites; the maps are the real in-game Town Maps for Kanto, Johto and Hoenn, with ROM-accurate wild-encounter data and route graphs. Base-town BFS proximity works in any region. Every parameter recomputes live. ★ Open source on GitHub · MIT · a fan project, not affiliated with Nintendo / Game Freak.
